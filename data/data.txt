OnlineRetail.csv is derived from the Online Retail Data Set of the UCI Machine Learning Repository

Source: http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/online+retail

The following modifications have been made to the data in preparation for this assignment
-Removed transactions with no customer number
-Removed all but 35 unique items with a preference for commonly purchased items
-Removed all but the top 473 customers by number of purchases
-Removed purchases with quantity >=10
-Reformatted the remaining information
